# Table Transpose Tool

Transposes ASCII tables like those output by MySQL.

## Example

        $ cat table.txt
        +----+----+----+----+----+----+----+----+
        |  a |  b |  c |  d |  e |  f |  g |  h |
        +----+----+----+----+----+----+----+----+
        | 11 | 12 | 13 | 14 | 15 | 16 | 17 | 18 |
        | 21 | 22 | 23 | 24 | 25 | 26 | 27 | 28 |
        +----+----+----+----+----+----+----+----+

        $ tt < table.txt
        +---+----+----+
        | a | 11 | 21 |
        +---+----+----+
        | b | 12 | 22 |
        | c | 13 | 23 |
        | d | 14 | 24 |
        | e | 15 | 25 |
        | f | 16 | 26 |
        | g | 17 | 27 |
        | h | 18 | 28 |
        +---+----+----+
